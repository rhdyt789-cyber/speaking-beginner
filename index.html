Speaking Exercise

Learning Material

Useful Expressions:

  • Hello, my name is ...
  • I am ... years old.
  • I live in ...
  • My hobby is ...
  • Nice to meet you.

Example:

Hello. My name is Anna. I am 18 years old. I live in Bali. My hobby is reading books. Nice to meet you.

Speaking Task

Record yourself answering the following questions:

  1. What is your name?
  2. How old are you?
  3. Where do you live?
  4. What is your hobby?
